Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is included in each lead?

Every lead includes: Homeowner's name, phone number/email, full address or postal code, specific service requested (removal, trimming, stump grinding, etc.), urgency level (standard or emergency), and submission timestamp. All from real property owners requesting quotes.

Are leads shared or exclusive to me?

Standard pricing ($2,000/50 leads): Leads are shared with up to 3 companies in your area - this keeps costs down. Exclusive option: Available at premium pricing - email us for exclusive rates. Most partners start with shared and see great ROI.

How are leads delivered?

Instantly via email the moment a homeowner submits. We also offer API integration for automatic CRM import. Average delivery time: under 30 seconds from submission.

Can I scale my volume up or down?

Absolutely. Pricing is per 50 leads. Want 50? Pay $2,000. Want 100? Pay $4,000. Want 250? Pay $10,000. Scale up or down monthly based on your capacity. Simple math.

Can I choose my coverage area?

Yes. Select specific cities, metro areas, states/provinces, or multi-region territories. Perfect for both single-location shops and regional operations.

What if I don't receive my full lead count?

We guarantee your purchased volume. If you buy 50 and only receive 40 in a month, we either roll the remaining 10 to next month or prorate your invoice. You never pay for leads you don't receive.

Do you work with franchises?

Yes. We have franchise packages where corporate purchases lead packages for franchisees. We can deliver to multiple locations with location-level reporting. Email us for franchise pricing.

What's the contract length?

Month-to-month. No long-term contracts. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice. We earn your business every month by delivering quality.

Can I resell these leads?

Yes. Marketing agencies and lead aggregators purchase wholesale and resell under their brand. Minimum 100 leads/month for reseller accounts. Email for wholesale pricing.

Do prices vary by country?

Base pricing is $2,000 per 50 leads across all countries (USD). Lead availability varies by market - UK, Canada, and Australia partners should email us to confirm current inventory in target regions.

How do I get started?

Email partners@gettreequotes.com with your company details, target coverage area, and desired monthly volume. We'll respond within 24 hours and can start delivering leads within 48-72 hours.
