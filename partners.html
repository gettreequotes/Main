Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a "qualified lead"?

A qualified lead includes the homeowner's name, phone number/email, location (city/postal code), specific service requested (removal, trimming, stump grinding, etc.), and whether it's an emergency. All leads come from property owners who filled out our form requesting quotes.

Are leads shared or exclusive?

Standard Package ($2,000/month): Leads are shared with up to 3 companies in your area. Premium Package ($4,000/month): 100% exclusive to you - no competition. Most partners start with standard and upgrade as they prove ROI.

How quickly are leads delivered?

Instantly. The moment a homeowner submits a request, you receive an email with their details. You can also integrate via API for automatic CRM import. Average time from submission to your inbox: under 30 seconds.

Can I choose specific cities or coverage areas?

Absolutely. You can select: (1) Specific cities (e.g., "Dallas only"), (2) Metro areas (e.g., "Greater Toronto"), (3) Entire states/provinces, or (4) Multi-region territories. Perfect for both single-location and regional operations.

What if I don't get 100 leads in a month?

We guarantee 100 leads monthly based on your selected coverage area. If lead volume is lower in a given month, we either: (1) Roll over unused leads to the next month, or (2) Prorate your invoice. You never pay for leads you don't receive.

Do you work with franchises?

Yes! We have special packages for franchises where corporate can purchase lead packages for franchisees. We can deliver leads to multiple locations with franchisee-level reporting. Email us for franchise pricing.

What's the contract length?

Month-to-month. No long-term contracts. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice. We believe in earning your business every month by delivering quality leads.

Can I pause my account seasonally?

Yes. Many partners pause during winter months (Nov-Feb in northern climates) and resume in spring. Just give us 15 days notice before your next billing cycle.

Do you offer lead reselling/white-label options?

Absolutely. Marketing agencies and lead aggregators can purchase wholesale and resell under their own brand. Minimum 200 leads/month for reseller packages. Email partners@gettreequotes.com for wholesale pricing.

Do pricing and terms differ by country?

Base pricing is consistent across all countries ($2,000 USD/month for 100 leads). However, lead volume availability varies by market. UK, Canada, and Australia partners should email us to discuss current inventory levels in their target regions.

How do I get started?

Email us at partners@gettreequotes.com with your company details, target coverage area, and desired monthly lead volume. We'll respond within 24 hours with next steps and can start delivering leads within 48-72 hours.
